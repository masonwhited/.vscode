Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to make your mom's signature dinner but she never answers the phone and you can't find the recipe? Welcome to Mom's Kitchen. A website made to collect all of your favorite childhood recipes in one easy to find place.

Audience

On this page I would like to once and for all write down all of my family's generational recipes for easy access and sustainability that will outlast the lost index cards in the attic.

Content

Home page

the main home page will contain information on what we at Mom's Kitchen hope to provide to those who visit our website with. Namely, an overview of the types of recipes we cover, the history of Mom's Kitchen, and possibly contact information for recipe clarification, but that is still to be determined.

Images for the Home page

Main Dishes

For a second page, I thought it would be nice to have that one cover 'Main Dishes'. Your casseroles, entrees, showstopper types of dishes. These would include the biscuits and gravy recipe, Aunt Pauline's casserole, Tatertot casserole, and Mason's Stroganoff. Of course, alongside the recipes, we'll need pictures and step by step instructions on how to make them, but they're all relatively simple.

Images for the Page 2

Desserts

For a third and final page, I was thinking we could make it about 'Desserts'. These include Whited Brownies (which will set you on the path to chocolatey diabetes), Chocolate coconut bars, and Hershey Bar Pie. Now I know they're all chocolate-based desserts, but that's what we got in the family, a bunch of chocoholics. Some of these are incredibly easy to make and I've actually used one in a school project where we have to give a demonstration speech in front of the class.
