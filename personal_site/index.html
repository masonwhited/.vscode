History of Mom's Kitchen

Mom's Kitchen started off as a place for me to preserve all those family recipes that have been passed down on index cards from generation to generation. This way I have them readily available for when I want to make one of them, but don't have the index cards on hand.

Types of Dishes

For the purpose of this site we'll be covering a few main dishes and desserts. That way you have enough to impress any date, coworker, or members of a family function. These meals will also help feed your family tasty foods on a budget while still providing their nutritional requirements.
